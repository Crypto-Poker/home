Game manual

All cards are divided into 5 qualities according to the card color, green - C, blue - B, purple - A, gold - S and black - SSS


Each card of Crypto poker game has unique skill effects. Use these skill effects for reasonable matching to make your battle group stronger.


In the crypto poker game, you can use up to 7 cards to form your battle group. At the same time, only one card can be configured for the same card in a card group. A reasonable lineup will make your battle easier.

  • Skill keyword list:

  • Dead language Ridicule
  • War roar Aegis
  • Anger Highly toxic
  • Rapidly Resurrection
  • Summon
Dead language

Skill effect triggered when a card dies.
Example:
Dead language: let our HP highest card + 2HP.
When the card with this skill effect dies, our HP highest card + 2HP.

Ridicule

Cards with this skill effect will be attacked first.
Example:
Spade 5 has Ridicule
Enemy cards will attack spade 5 first.

War roar

Cards with this skill effect will trigger the corresponding effect at the beginning of the battle.
Example:
Battle roar ï¼šour spades gain + 1 / + 1
At the beginning of the battle, this card will add 1 attack and 1 HP to all our spades.

Aegis

A card with this skill effect can resist any form of damage once.
Example:
Spade 8 has Aegis
Any skill or attack (including poison) caused to spade 8 will be invalid, but the effect of this skill can only be triggered once.

Anger

The card with this effect will trigger the corresponding skill effect if it does not die on the first attack.
Example:
Anger: gain + 5HP.
When the card is attacked but not dead, the card gains + 5 health. This skill effect can only be triggered once.

Highly toxic

The card with this skill effect ignores the enemy's HP and will die immediately after being hurt by this card.
Example:
Spade J is highly toxic.
If spade J attacks any unit, the unit will die immediately ( Aegis can resist one damage).

Rapidly

Cards with this skill effect will attack twice when attacking
Example:
Spade 6 has Rapidly.
Spade 6 can launch two attacks in a row (except when he dies after the first attack)

Resurrection

Cards with this ability effect will be resurrected with basic attributes after death.
Example:
Spade 7 has resurrection.
Spade 7 will be revived with his basic attribute value after being hurt and dying (other attribute values superimposed will not be counted)

Summon

The card of this skill effect can summon another card when triggered.
Example:
Spade 4 dead language: summon a spade 3.
Trigger the skill effect after spade 4 dies and summon a spade 3 card.
